# Business Sales Performance Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview
This project analyzes retail sales data to identify revenue trends, top-selling products, customer behavior, and regional sales performance. The dashboard provides actionable insights to support data-driven business decisions.

## Tools Used
- Power BI
- Excel/CSV
- DAX
- Data Cleaning & Transformation

## Key KPIs
- Total Revenue: 10.2M
- Total Orders: 21K
- Total Customers: 4K
- Average Order Value: 0.50K

## Key Insights
- Revenue showed significant growth during the last quarter of the year.
- The United Kingdom contributes approximately 85% of total revenue.
- A small number of products generate the majority of sales.
- Sales peak during November and December due to seasonal demand.

## Recommendations
- Focus marketing efforts on top-performing products.
- Expand operations in international markets.
- Introduce product bundles and loyalty programs.
- Leverage seasonal promotions to maximize revenue.
